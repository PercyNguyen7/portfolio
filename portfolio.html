About Percy

Web Design & Developer

Vinh Tuan Dat Nguyen , also known as Percy, is a third year student in Computation Arts at Concordia. Born in Vietnam, Percy weathered snowstorms and found his new home in Montreal in 2015.

Percy’s works often involve interactive narratives that explore themes of choices, morality and humanity.
